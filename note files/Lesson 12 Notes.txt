TITLE: The Modulus Operator
DESC: The modulus operatore looks like this: 15%2. Basically it is asking for the remainder from the first number divided by the second. So in this case, 15%2 would be 1 because 2 goes into 15 7 times, with a remainder of 1. 2 x 7 = 14, 15 - 14 = 1. Other examples:<br>
<br>
4 % 3 = 1<br>
15 % 11 = 4<br>
25 % 4 = 1<br>
